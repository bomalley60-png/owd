Canadian Web Services

With the current climate in Canada, and a growing distrust of our neighbours to the south, many of us are now opting for Canadian products in stores, and Canadian alternatives for out day to day needs. A plethora of online services exist to help you develop your online presence, in conjunction with what I offer as a solely Canadian owned business. I have listed below I variety of businesses and services that can help you with your digital goals.

Funio

Funio.com is a Web Hosting and Domain Name registrar website based out of Verdun, Quebec, offering a local option to the likes of GoDaddy and Squarespace. I personally used Funio.com to register my Domain Name, and the process was streamlined and affordable, with a .CA extension coming in at $13.99 / year, .COM costing $23.99 / year, and .NET $24.99 / year. They have multiple other extensions available at various price points depending on your needs, including .INFO, .ORG, .SITE, and more. They offer web hosting for an additional cost, varying upon which service you feel most comfortable with. Alternatively the ability to run through a repository to prevent any additional costs is also available, and very easy to achieve through documentation provided by your preferred host.

Worktree

Another Quebec based service, Worktree is a Canadian owned code forge and DevOps platform that is a full-featured Git collaboration with a solid documentation page to help those new to using repositories for their coding needs.
